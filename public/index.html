Youth Suicide In Sweden

10 - 24 years old

In 2018, 171 youths committed suicide in Sweden.

62% of them were boys.

Why the big difference between boys and girls.

Girls are more likely to attempt suicide, while boys have higher rates of lethality. source

“Men tend to choose more violent and immediately lethal means, which increases their likelihood of dying. Women tend to choose less lethal means, which increases their chance of survival.” source

“Men may also choose these methods because they’re more intent on completing the act.” source

“Teenage boys are less likely to talk about suicide before committing the act. Boys often keep their feelings to themselves and also commit suicide more often.” source
